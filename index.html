What Is Money?

08.08.2021 13:50
What is money? Is it a medium of exchange? Is it a convenient tool we all decide to use to carry out transactions? I would argue it is not a medium of exchange and it is not a convenient tool we use to perform transactions. Instead, I believe that money is a measurement device of value. In other words, it is a unit of account. Like we use the Fahrenheit scale to measure temperature, we use money to measure value. Read more...
